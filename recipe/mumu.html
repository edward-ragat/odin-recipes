TOLAI-MUMU

The Tolai mumu

The Tolai mumu, is a specially made dish to be eaten at any time you wish, below outline its preparation and cooking steps.

INGREDIENTS
  • Peeled Energy Foods
    • A bundle peeled taros
    • A bundle peeled bananas
    • 15 to 20 sweet potatos peeled
    • 15 peeled ripe java bananas (optional)
  • 200gram grated coconut
  • Seasonings
  • 900gram chicken pieces
STEPS
  1. Prepare Energy foods peeled earlier on
  2. Prepare cut banana leafs in pot shaped manner
  3. Placed peeled energies, with chicken pieces neatly inside the banana leafs
  4. milk with grated coconut and seasoned thoroughly
  5. parcel and gently placed the uncooked food parcel in an earth oven
  6. Covered completely with hot stones for 4-6 hours approximately
