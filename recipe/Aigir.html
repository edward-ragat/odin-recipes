AIGIR DISH

INGREDIENTS
  • 5 to 10 bundles variuos types of greens
  • 50 gram sliced carrots
  • 10 gram gated yellow ginger
  • 10 gram grated garlic
  • 50 gram meshed tomatoes
  • 1.5 ltr coconut milk
  • 1.4 kg chicken broiler pieces or any edible protein; optional
STEPS
  1. heat stones over a fire
    1. the fire must be 120 deg celcius
  2. place 3 or 4 hot stones inside cooker pot
  3. Place all the chicken pieces or any edible protein into the cooker pot and pour 500ml of coconut milk
  4. add some more hot stones into the cooker pot and also add all greens into the cooker pot
  5. pour remaing 1ltr milk and add all spices
  6. allow to cook on steam by closing the cooker pot lid
  7. After 2min open cooker pot lid and allow to cool
